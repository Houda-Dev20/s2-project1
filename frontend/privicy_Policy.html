At LifeDrop, your privacy is as vital as the donations you make. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform to connect blood donors with patients in need.

01

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your full name, phone number, email address, blood type, and location. When you register as a donor or submit a donation request, we also collect hospital name and urgency status. We may collect device information and usage data to improve app performance.

02

How we use your information

Your data is used solely to match donors with patients, send relevant notifications about nearby requests, and ensure the safety of all parties involved. We do not use your data for advertising purposes. Blood type and location data are only shared with matched users during an active donation request.

03

Location data

LifeDrop uses your approximate location to find nearby patients and hospitals. Location access is only active when you open the app or enable notifications. We never share precise location data with third parties and do not store location history beyond 30 days.
