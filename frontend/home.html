Can you donate blood?

Check if you're eligible to save a life

You can donate if...

  • are 18 years or older

  • You weigh at least 50 kg

  • You are in good general health and feeling well

  • It has been 8 weeks since last blood donation

  • Your blood pressure and iron levels are within normal range

You cannot donate if...

  • You have a cold, flu, or fever in the past 48 hours

  • You are pregnant or breastfeeding

  • You have taken antibiotics in the past 24 hours

  • You have certain chronic conditions

  • You have had hepatitis or certain infections
